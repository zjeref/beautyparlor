Why Choose Us?

From 1 salon to over 600 salons across UK, all it took was one woman’s passion. This drove D & G to become UK’s No.1 hair and beauty salon. At D & G, we believe in financial independence for women and have empowered 300+ women to become entrepreneurs in the past 16 years. Our dream is to create a housewife-free UK, where women are encouraged to earn their living by pursuing their passion. By 2025, D & G aims to create 1000 salons, empower 1000 women entrepreneurs and create 50,000 jobs, because there is no better style statement than standing on your own feet.
